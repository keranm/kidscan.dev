What is this?

This site aims to become an online resource dedicated to the discovery of clubs, communities, groups, projects, online tools, toys and gadgets that encourage experimentation and discovery of coding, development, design and usage of tech in life.

It is very early days here, so stick around - we're aiming to create something pretty cool.

Get Involved

  • Code & Development

    We're looking for clubs and communities that run workshops, host meetups and teach kids. From coding to design thinking, agile process to entrepreneurship.

  • Online Resources

    We're looking for online tutorials, videos and webinars that teach kids. From coding to design thinking, agile process to entrepreneurship.

  • Gadgets, tech & toys

    We love a good gadget and there are so many amazing tools and physical devices that foster discovery of logic, nuture programming and allow kids simple ways to interact and impact their world.
